🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Process
Gatekeeper AI processes messages from SMS, WhatsApp, and Email that you explicitly connect to our service. We do NOT collect or store personal information beyond what is necessary to sort your messages.

2. How We Use Your Data
Your messages are processed solely to classify them as URGENT, IMPORTANT, or NORMAL. We do NOT read your messages for any other purpose. Your data is NEVER sold, shared, or used for advertising.

3. Data Encryption
All messages are encrypted before storage (AES-256). Only the AI has temporary access to sort your messages. The AI does NOT remember or store your messages after sorting.

4. Data Retention
Messages are automatically deleted after 30 days. You can change this to 7, 14, 60, or 90 days in settings. Upon account deletion, all data is permanently erased within 30 days.

5. No Human Access
No Gatekeeper AI employee — including the founder — can read your messages. This is enforced by technical encryption and database security.

6. Third-Party Services
We use OpenAI for AI processing (zero-data-retention), Supabase for encrypted storage, and Twilio for SMS. Each is contractually bound to protect your data.

7. Your Rights
You have the right to: Request a copy of all your data, Request deletion of all your data, Export your data to another service, Cancel your subscription at any time.

8. Contact
Data Protection Officer: support@gatekeeperai.co.zw
